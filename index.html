About

Hi! My name is Merjem, but I also go by Vuko. I'm a Bosnian artist and illustrator. My work has spanned across many mediums but the most commonly used are watercolors and digital illustration. I love creating art that expresses the nature and all the wonders it hides. Hope you have a fun time hanging here OwO.

Spirare Natura

Spirare Natura is our jewellery line focused on conserving wildlife. Each of Spirare Natura jewellery pieces, made carefully with immense amount of love, is unique. With each piece of jewellery you carry a small piece of art along with a meaning special to you. Like in nature where everything has its place and is unexampled but beautiful, you too have your own unique melody. Express yourself, be one with the nature.
